Digital Logo Designing

  • One team must comprise of 2 participants.
  • 2 teams allowed per institution.
  • Topic/Theme will be given on the spot.
  • At the beginning, 15 minutes will be given to build a rough sketch of logo on an A4 size sheet. No internet access will be allowed during these 20 minutes.
  • Following that, participants will have 60 minutes to recreate their initial logo sketch using any one of the permitted design tools. Internet access will be allowed.
  • Permitted Design Tools: Google Slides, Canva, Adobe Photoshop, Corel Draw, MSOffice, Paint
  • Size: 800 * 800 pixels
  • Participants are encouraged to bring their own laptops, although computers will be available for use during the event.
  • In case participants are unable to bring their own laptops, we will only provide Paint, Canva and Google Slides for their use.

Web Wizards Rules

  • One team must comprise of 2 participants.
  • 2 teams allowed per institution.
  • Theme will be given on the spot. All the assets will be provided.
  • Time Limit: 90 minutes
  • Compulsory Languages- HTML & CSS
  • Optional Language- JavaScript
  • Permitted Platform- Any offline code/text editor.
  • Internet access will not be allowed.
  • Participants are encouraged to bring their own laptops, although computers will be available for use during the event.
  • In case participants are unable to bring their own laptops, we will only provide Notepad and VS Code for their use.
  • Only 1 device is allowed per team.

Digital Poster Creation Rules

  • One team must comprise of 2 participants.
  • 2 teams allowed per institution.
  • Topic/Theme will be given on the spot.
  • Time Limit: 60 minutes
  • Permitted Design Tools: Canva, Adobe Photoshop, Corel Draw, MS-Office, Paint
  • Internet access will be allowed only for using Canva and Google Slides.
  • Size: 1080 * 1920 pixels (width * height)
  • Participants are encouraged to bring their own laptops, although computers will be available for use during the event.
  • In case participants are unable to bring their own laptops, we will only provide Paint and Canva for their use.
